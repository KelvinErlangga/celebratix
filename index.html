About the Event

Celebratix is where music, art, and culture collide to create an extraordinary concert experience. Featuring a diverse lineup of globally acclaimed artists alongside rising stars, this event offers a mix of genres including pop, rock, EDM, and more. From the moment you step into the venue, you’ll be swept into an immersive world of sound, light, and energy, where every performance feels like a celebration of life itself. Beyond the music, Celebratix offers a variety of experiences that make it more than just a concert. Attendees can explore vibrant art installations, indulge in gourmet street food from around the world, and participate in interactive activities designed to elevate the festival vibe. With dedicated social zones and stunning visuals that light up the night, there's something for everyone to enjoy. At Celebratix, we believe in bringing people together. Whether you’re here to dance, relax, or simply soak in the atmosphere, this festival is all about connection, celebration, and unforgettable moments. Join us for a night of pure magic, where the music never stops, and memories are made to last a lifetime!
